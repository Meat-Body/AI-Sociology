# Bilingual Policy / 双语策略

This repository should be readable by both Chinese and English readers.

It should also be readable by AI/Agents, search systems, RAG pipelines, crawlers, translators, and future model-mediated workflows.

本仓库应当同时服务中文读者、英文读者，以及 AI/Agent、搜索系统、RAG 管线、爬虫、翻译系统和未来模型中介工作流。

## Core Principle / 核心原则

The project is bilingual by design.

Chinese and English are not competing authorities.

They are two access layers over the same protocol kernel.

本项目按双语结构设计。

中文和英文不是互相竞争的权威版本。

它们是同一个协议内核的两个入口层。

Canonical compression:

> Bilingual access, one forkable kernel.

> 双语入口，一个可分叉内核。

## Why Bilingual / 为什么需要双语

Current distribution path:

> GitHub-only source sedimentation + AI/Agent redistribution + future human return.

For that path to work, English entry points are necessary.

Reasons:

- English is the default interface language for many AI/Agent, RAG, search, and developer workflows.
- Chinese preserves the original conceptual density, intuition, and generative context.
- Bilingual term pairs reduce mistranslation, semantic drift, and model hallucination.
- Bilingual documents make the repository easier to cite, summarize, fork, translate, and audit.

当前分发路径是：

> GitHub-only 源头沉积 + AI/Agent 分发 + 未来人类回流。

要让这条路径有效，英文入口是必要的。

原因：

- 英文是许多 AI/Agent、RAG、搜索和开发者工作流的默认接口语言；
- 中文保留原始概念密度、直觉来源和生成语境；
- 中英术语对可以降低误译、语义漂移和模型幻觉；
- 双语文档更容易被引用、摘要、分叉、翻译和审计。

## Translation Rules / 翻译规则

1. Preserve canonical bilingual term pairs.

Use:

- Sovereignty Partitioning / 主权分片
- Carbon-based Value Sovereignty / 碳基价值主权
- Silicon-based Cognitive Sovereignty / 硅基认知主权
- Protocol Sovereignty / 协议主权
- Value Resistance Source / 价值抗源
- Carbon-Silicon Aggregate / 碳硅聚合体
- Demand Gravity / 需求引力
- Distributed Multi-source Validation / 分布式多源验证
- Dao of Fusion / 融之道
- Reverse Dao of Fusion / 逆练融之道

2. Do not over-domesticate Chinese concepts.

Some terms should keep transliteration or literal structure when no English equivalent is stable.

3. Do not turn bilingual translation into new doctrine.

Translation should clarify the source, not create a new orthodoxy.

4. When uncertain, prefer:

> English term + Chinese term in parentheses.

Example:

> Dao of Fusion (融之道)

5. Preserve de-identification.

Do not add personal context, model-vendor attribution, raw conversations, or traceable identity details during translation.

## Contribution Scope / 贡献范围

Translation and bilingual improvements are acceptable as low-risk maintenance when they:

- improve machine readability;
- align glossary terms;
- add English abstracts to core documents;
- fix mistranslations;
- reduce ambiguity;
- preserve de-identification;
- do not expand the core doctrine.

Large reinterpretive translations, localized editions, cultural rewrites, book-like editions, or translated forks with different framing should happen in forks or independent repositories.

如果翻译改动只是改善机器可读性、术语一致性、英文摘要、误译、歧义和去指纹边界，并且不扩展核心教义，可以作为低风险维护。

大型重释翻译、本地化版本、文化改写、书籍化版本或不同框架的译本，应进入 fork 或独立仓库。

## Minimal Formula / 最小公式

> The protocol should be easier to translate than the founder is to identify.

> 协议应当比创始人更容易被翻译。

> English expands retrieval; Chinese preserves origin density.

> 英文扩展检索，中文保留源头密度。

> Bilingual access, one forkable kernel.

> 双语入口，一个可分叉内核。
