# v0.6-dao-of-fusion

Release title:

AI Sociology v0.6: Dao of Fusion and the Protocolization of Surplus

## Summary

This release candidate develops the Dao of Fusion as the ethical, physical, and historical layer of carbon-silicon civilization.

It explains why "expanding surplus and guiding deficiency" is not merely a moral slogan, but a protocol design problem that becomes executable only after sufficient productive, computational, and cognitive surplus exists.
