AI Sociology v0.8: Return Flow, Activation Threshold, and First-Principles Reading

## Summary

This release candidate extends the Dao of Fusion by clarifying how the Way of Humans, the Way of Heaven, and the Dao of Fusion can be understood as a temporal sequence rather than isolated moral categories.

It also adds a first-principles reading layer: similar structural constraints may be independently discovered through ancient texts, craft practice, modern protocols, open-source systems, business loops, or carbon-silicon collaboration.
