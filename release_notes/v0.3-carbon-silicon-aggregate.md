# v0.3-carbon-silicon-aggregate

Release title:

AI Sociology v0.3: Carbon-Silicon Aggregates and Satellite Systems

## Summary

This release expands the subject layer of AI Sociology.

It argues that the future action unit is not always a standalone human or standalone AI system, but may increasingly become a long-coupled carbon-silicon aggregate.
