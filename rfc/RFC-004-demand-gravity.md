# RFC-004: Demand Gravity

Status: `Draft`

## Summary

Describe how high-penetration demand attracts resources, computation, institutions, and coordination under post-scarcity automation conditions.

## Motivation

As automation and compute abundance expand, resource allocation may increasingly depend on the quality of demand definition rather than access to labor or capital alone.

## Questions

- What makes a demand gravitational rather than merely fashionable?
- How does public gain differ from private extraction?
- How should distributed validation be operationalized?

## Open Issues

- Pseudo-gravity driven by hype cycles.
- Measurement difficulty across time horizons.
- Interaction with markets, states, and platform ecosystems.
