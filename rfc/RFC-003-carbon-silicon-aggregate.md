# RFC-003: Carbon-Silicon Aggregate

Status: `Draft`

## Summary

Define the carbon-silicon aggregate as an emergent action structure formed by a human subject and long-coupled AI systems.

## Motivation

The future unit of action may increasingly be a human plus memory, agents, models, and execution interfaces rather than a standalone human or standalone model.

## Questions

- What qualifies as a stable aggregate?
- How should memory, agency, and override rights be structured?
- What are the main lock-in risks?

## Open Issues

- Old-profile lock-in.
- Portability across model providers and local systems.
- Protocol safeguards for self-revision.
