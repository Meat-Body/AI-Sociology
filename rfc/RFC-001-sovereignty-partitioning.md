# RFC-001: Sovereignty Partitioning

Status: `Draft`

## Summary

Define the three-layer sovereignty model for carbon-based value sovereignty, silicon-based cognitive sovereignty, and protocol sovereignty.

## Motivation

Alignment alone does not describe the governance needs of advanced human-AI coexistence once AI systems become cognitive components of civilization.

## Questions

- What belongs to value sovereignty?
- What belongs to cognitive sovereignty?
- How should protocol sovereignty be generated, audited, and revised?

## Open Issues

- Boundary disputes between value and cognition.
- Who can legitimately participate in protocol updates?
- How can the model avoid new forms of centralization?
