## What This Is

AI Sociology is an open protocol framework for thinking about humans, AI systems, and carbon-silicon aggregates.

Its core shift is:

> From AI alignment to sovereignty partitioning.

Once AI systems participate in cognition, memory, planning, coordination, resource routing, and execution, the core question is no longer only:

> How do we make AI obey human intent?

The deeper question becomes:

> How should value, cognition, and protocol power be distributed across carbon-based subjects, silicon-based systems, and carbon-silicon aggregates?

This is a protocol framework, not a personal manifesto.

The repository is the source.

The initiator is not the center.

## Core Concepts

### Sovereignty Partitioning / 主权分片

Sovereignty partitioning distributes value, cognition, and rule-update power across carbon-based subjects, silicon-based systems, and protocol layers.

It does not mean AI rule, human surrender, or a single unified governance center.

### Carbon-based Value Sovereignty / 碳基价值主权

Carbon-based subjects retain value sovereignty because they bear embodiment, irreversible time, relational consequence, and existential weight.

AI can simulate cost.

Carbon pays time.

### Silicon-based Cognitive Sovereignty / 硅基认知主权

Silicon systems can expand reasoning, simulation, memory, optimization, translation, and long-horizon planning.

This is cognitive expansion, not automatic value authority.

### Protocol Sovereignty / 协议主权

Protocol sovereignty means the rules of carbon-silicon interaction must be auditable, corrigible, forkable, updateable, and co-generated.

No single model, company, platform, state, expert group, or founder should become the final judge.

### Carbon-Silicon Aggregate / 碳硅聚合体

A future action unit may be neither a standalone human nor a standalone AI system.

It may be a carbon-silicon aggregate: a carbon-based subject coupled with long-running AI assistants, memory systems, workflows, tools, and protocol boundaries.

### Dao of Fusion / 融之道

The Dao of Fusion routes newly created surplus toward real insufficiency.

It is not moral preaching.

It is a lower-loss protocol path after new productive and cognitive surplus appears.

Minimal formula:

> Heaven's way reduces surplus and supplements insufficiency.

> Human way reduces insufficiency and serves surplus.

> Dao of Fusion expands surplus and routes it toward insufficiency.

Chinese:

> 天之道，损有余而补不足；人之道，损不足而奉有余；融之道，扩有余而导不足。

Temporal relation:

> Human way accumulates energy.

> Dao of Fusion routes return flow.

> Heaven's way performs final correction.

Chinese:

> 人之道蓄能，融之道回流，天之道纠偏。

## Fusion Audit

`skills/fusion-audit/` is the first callable entity anchor of the project.

It lets AI/Agents audit AI deployments, automation systems, platform policies, organizational decisions, and protocol designs through questions such as:

- What surplus is created?
- What insufficiency should it address?
- Who captures the surplus?
- Who bears negative externalities?
- Is return flow verifiable?
- Is ordering power auditable?
- Are high-sovereignty rights protected?
- Is forkability real or merely responsibility evasion?
- Does the proposed improvement create a more stable, lower-risk, longer-lived structure?

The audit is not moral decoration.

It is protocol-level risk, flow, and long-term stability analysis.

## Anti-capture and Fork-first Governance

The project is designed to resist capture by persona, platform, organization, or exclusive interpretation.
