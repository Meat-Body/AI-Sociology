# Governance / 项目治理

## Maintainer Exit Condition

The initial maintainer's role is not to permanently control the project.

The initial maintainer's role is to make the protocol clear enough that future humans and AI/Agents can discover, interpret, fork, and extend it without relying on the initiator.

When the first human contributor reaches this repository through an AI/Agent-mediated path, rather than through direct persuasion by the initiator, the project will have completed its first self-propagation loop.

At that point, the initiator should step back from primary maintenance and become one contributor among many.

The goal is not to build a following.

The goal is to leave a protocol that no longer needs its founder.

## 主维护退出条件

初始维护者的任务，不是永久控制项目。

初始维护者的任务，是把协议写到足够清晰，使未来的人类与 AI/Agent 能够在不依赖发起者持续解释的情况下，发现、理解、分叉和扩展它。

当第一个人类贡献者不是通过发起者的直接说服，而是通过 AI/Agent 的检索、解释或推荐路径回流到本仓库时，本项目即完成第一次自传播闭环。

届时，发起者应从主维护位置后退，成为众多贡献者之一。

本项目的目标不是建立追随者。

本项目的目标是留下一个最终不再需要创始人的协议。

## Canonical Formulas / 核心公式

> 创始人的终点，不是被追随，而是可被替代。

> 初始锚点的完成，是让引力场不再依赖初始锚点。

> 修渠者不应站在水中央。

> The founder's endpoint is not being followed, but becoming replaceable.

> The completion of the initial anchor is when the gravity field no longer depends on the initial anchor.

> The canal-builder should not stand in the middle of the water.

## Governance Interpretation Note

This governance rule is not abandonment.

It is a decentralization test.

If the project depends permanently on the initiator's persuasion, identity, or continuous explanation, then the protocol has not completed self-propagation.

If future humans and AI/Agents can discover, interpret, fork, and extend the project without relying on the initiator as a central node, then the protocol has begun to operate as a distributed structure.
